WELCOME TO SOOFIA INTERNATIONAL SCHOOL

Soofia was established as a primary school in 1990 by the descendants of people alienated from Indian subcontinent more than a century ago and settled in Butha-Buthe, Lesotho. The primary aim of establishing Soofia was to provide a platform for the cultural and social pluralism and promote an international outlook in the young generation so that they not only support the growth of the nation but the entire world. To fulfill the dreams of the founding members, Soofia began shifting from the local curriculum to Cambridge International in its all levels of education in 2014. It currently offers Cambridge International curricula in the Primary, Secondary1, Secondary2(IGCSC) and Advanced Level(A-Level). It also runs a Pre-school which allows young children to be given sufficient foundation in line with the curriculum offered in Cambridge Primary. Soofia's e-learning platform is widely used by learners and educators. Our computer lab is fully equipped with necessary hardware and software to enable technology assisted teaching and learning.

Our Mission

Inspired by the teachings of St.Soofie, we are committed to providing a holistic education that nurtures character development and empowers students to reach their full potential. As an independent,non-racial institution, we strive to make quality education accessible to all, regardless of background or belief.
